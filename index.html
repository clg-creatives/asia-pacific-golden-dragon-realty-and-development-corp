About Us

Integrated Real Estate Excellence from Subic Bay to the World

Asia Pacific Golden Dragon Realty and Development Corporation is a Philippine stock corporation registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission, strategically headquartered at the Subic Bay Freeport Zone (SBMA) in Olongapo City, Zambales.

From our roots in property acquisition, we have evolved into a full-spectrum real estate enterprise with authorized capital of One Hundred Million Pesos (₱100,000,000.00). Our operations span property development, management, leasing, sales, and capital formation — serving local communities, government agencies, foreign investors, and institutional partners.

We are committed to sustainable urbanization, affordable housing under the 4PH program, PEZA-registered economic zones, and the future establishment of a Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT).
